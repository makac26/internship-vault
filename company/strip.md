# Stripe

## Culture
Fast-paced, high ownership, engineers are expected to think like product managers...

## Work Style
Mostly async, deep work is respected, meetings are minimal...

## What They Value
Curiosity, attention to detail, people who read the docs before asking...

## Intern Experience
Interns ship real features, paired with a mentor, expected to present at end of term...

## Vibe
Intense but collaborative, not cutthroat. People are very smart but approachable.
